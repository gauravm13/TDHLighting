TDH Lighting

Established in 2009, Theatre Dimmer Hardware (TDH Lighting) has grown into one of India's leading manufacturers, wholesalers, and traders of professional stage and architectural lighting solutions.

We specialize in DMX splitters, electronic dimmers, dimmer racks, sharpy lights, LED spotlights, stage lights, and theater profile lights, delivering reliable performance for entertainment venues, studios, and auditoriums across the country.

At TDH Lighting, innovation and quality are at the heart of everything we do. Our products are crafted using cutting-edge technology and precision engineering, ensuring superior durability, safety, and control.

Supported by a team of skilled professionals and trusted vendors, we are committed to providing customized lighting solutions that meet the unique creative and technical needs of our clients.

With over a decade of industry expertise, TDH Lighting continues to illuminate experiences—from theatrical productions to large-scale events—with intelligent, energy-efficient, and high-performance lighting systems designed to bring every stage to life.

"Bringing innovation, precision, and brilliance to every beam of light."
