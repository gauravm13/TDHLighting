About TDHLight

Established in 2009, Theatre Dimmer Hardware (TDH) is a pioneer in professional lighting solutions. We are a leading manufacturer, wholesaler, and trader of DMX Splitters, Electronic Dimmers, Dimmer Racks, Sharpy Lights, LED Spotlights, Stage Lights, and Theater Profile Lights.

Our products are built with precision and innovation, supported by vendors employing the latest machinery and technology to deliver unmatched quality and reliability.

Our Promise

At TDHLight, we don’t just build lighting equipment — we create experiences. Every product reflects our commitment to performance, sustainability, and innovation in the entertainment industry.

What We Offer

  • Stage Lighting Fixtures: Moving heads, profiles, washes, and beams for dynamic performances.

  • LED & Intelligent Solutions: DMX, RDM, wireless energy-efficient systems.
