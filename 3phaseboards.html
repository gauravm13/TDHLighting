Phase Boards

TDH 3 Phase Boards are engineered for reliable and efficient power distribution across event venues, studios, and large installations. Each unit combines safety, performance, and flexibility — ensuring consistent current delivery even under demanding conditions.

  • Equipped with high-quality MCCB, MCB, and Isolators for maximum protection.
  • Multiple socket configurations — 32A, 63A, and 125A outputs with Schuko connectors.
  • Designed with indicators, power-safe circuits, and optional AVF meters for monitoring.
  • Precision-built in mild steel enclosures — available in black, grey, or white finish.
