Our Journey

Founded in 2009, TDH Lighting began with a simple vision—to bring innovation and precision to the world of professional lighting. We had no big setup, no grand funding, just a handful of passionate engineers determined to redefine how light transforms spaces. Our early days were filled with experimentation, long hours, and a relentless drive to turn ideas into products that inspire.

Today, that spark has evolved into a fully equipped 100 sq ft manufacturing facility, powered by advanced technology and a skilled team of over 50 professionals. From stage and architectural lighting to custom solutions for creative environments, we’ve built a reputation for reliability, performance, and design excellence. Every fixture we produce reflects our commitment to craftsmanship and innovation.

TDH Lighting is more than just a manufacturer—it’s a story of passion, perseverance, and progress. We continue to push boundaries, lighting up stages, experiences, and dreams across the globe.
